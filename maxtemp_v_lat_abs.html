Temperature by Absolute Latitude

Relationship of Temperature and Absolute Latitude Position: This graphic was developed to show the linear Relationship between proximity to equator (by using the absolute value of the lattitude from zero, rather than signed values). Because we know, astronomically, the Earth's equator is both closest to the sun and also least prone to the tilt of the Earth. Indeed, unlike the non-absolute values, the linear relationship reveals itself readily. Interestingly, the effect is not as significant as one might expect, although the bias of cities in the northern hemisphere and the fixed-date of sampling likely do not reflect this relationship exactly.
