Wind Speed by Latitude

Windspeed also has no immediate trends based on latitude except that there appears to be a general rise in wind speed when traveling further north in the hemisphere. This could be due to the terrain influencing wind speed as steppes, tundras, and other flatlands would experience higher wind speeds. As with humidity and cloud coverage, wind speed is a function of a more complex system than temperature, which is based solely on radiation from the sun (and to a much lesser extent georadiation).

We should all be thankful the mean wind speed falls within the 0-5mph range, otherwise Earth would not be a very pleasant place to inhabit!
